Who We Are

Designing timeless spaces with creativity, precision and purpose.

Ahmed Hamid Architecture is an independent studio dedicated to elegant, functional and contemporary spaces. Every project is developed with attention to detail, architectural identity and the needs of the people who will live in it.

From residential villas to commercial buildings, interiors and 3D visualization — the goal is the same: turn an idea into a space that works, and a space worth remembering.
