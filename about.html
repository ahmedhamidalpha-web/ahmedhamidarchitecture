Who We Are

Designing timeless spaces with creativity, precision and purpose.

Ahmed Hamid Architecture is an independent studio based in Khartoum, Sudan, dedicated to elegant, functional and contemporary spaces. The studio is newly founded, built around one architect's hands-on involvement in every project — from the first sketch to the final drawing set.

From residential villas to commercial buildings, interiors and 3D visualization, every project is developed with attention to detail, architectural identity, and the real needs of the people who will use the space.

  • Modern architectureContemporary design with a clear identity.
  • Functional planningSmart circulation and efficient spaces.
  • Photorealistic visualizationSee the project before it's built.
  • Interior designInteriors as considered as the shell around them.
